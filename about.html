About Image Compression Pro

Image Compression Pro is a free online tool designed to help users compress images and convert images to PDF easily, securely, and quickly.

All processing happens directly in your browser. We do not upload or store your images on any server.

Why Use Our Tool?

  • Fast and free image compression
  • No registration required
  • Privacy-focused (client-side processing)
  • Works on mobile and desktop

This website is built as a learning and productivity project and will continue to improve over time.
